4 Others

4.1 Model Aeronautics (aka Aeromodelling)

From 2009 to 2014, he played for the Xiamen Aeromodelling Team (provincial-level team) and the Fujian Provincial Aeromodelling Team, and successively studied under Lin Qin, Li Shaosheng, Zeng Guosheng and other well-known coaches. He won more than 20 national and provincial awards and was awarded the honorary title of second-level athlete by the General Administration of Sport of the P.R. China. From 2013 to 2016, he successively served as the coach of Xiamen No. 2 Experimental Primary School and Xiamen Youth Palace Aeromodelling Team. As the head coach, he led the team to win more than 70 awards at various levels.
